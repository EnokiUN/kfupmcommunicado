Official Announcement: Qataif

OFFICIAL ANNOUNCEMENT.03/06/2024

Student Ambassadors Abo Altalahmeh and ENokiUN have finally reached an agreement w/KFUPM restaurant regarding the introduction of the highly anticipated ramadan delicacy, Qataif. After a long sought winded discussion, the ambassadors were able to secure a contract, starting Sunday, 9th of Ramadan almwafig 9th of March (what a coincedence) that enforces the serving of both Jubna and Jooz Qataif at Fatoor in the restaurant 3ala barakit illah.
